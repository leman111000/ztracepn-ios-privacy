## How to get help faster
When you contact support, please include:
- iPhone model and iOS version
- DexPN app version
- What you were trying to do (connect, change server, etc.)
- The exact error text (a screenshot is OK)
- Whether it happens on Wi-Fi, LTE/5G, or both

Please do **not** send passwords or payment card details.

---

## Common troubleshooting

### VPN does not connect
- Switch between Wi-Fi and cellular data
- Try a different server/region (if available)
- Force close the app and reopen it
- Reboot your device
- Check that VPN permission prompts were accepted

### Connected but no internet
- Try another server/region
- Disable other VPN apps (they can conflict)
- Reboot the device and reconnect

### App crashes or behaves unexpectedly
- Reinstall the app
- Contact support with crash steps and your iOS/app version

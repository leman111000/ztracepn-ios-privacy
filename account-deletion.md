# Account Deletion — ZTracePn (iOS)

**Last updated:** 2026-01-19  
**Support:** @DexNet_Support_bot

This page explains how to request deletion of information associated with you.

---

## How to request deletion
Send a deletion request to support:

**@ZTracePn_Support_bot**

In your message, include:
- A clear statement that you want deletion (for example: “Delete my data”),
- Your Telegram username (and any other identifiers you used with support),
- The approximate date you started using DexPN (optional but helpful).

---

## What will be deleted
After confirming the request, we will delete or anonymize, where applicable:
- Support conversations and attachments you provided (where technically possible),
- Service records associated with your identifier (where applicable).

---

## Verification
To protect you from unauthorized deletion requests, we may ask for additional verification steps.

---

## Retention exceptions
Some limited data may be retained if required by law or necessary to protect the service from fraud/abuse. Any retained data will be minimized and protected.

---

## Questions
Contact support: **@ZTracePn_Support_bot**

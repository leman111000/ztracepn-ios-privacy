# Privacy Policy — ZTracePn (iOS)

**Effective date:** 2026-01-19  
**Publisher:** ZTracePn  
**Support:** @ZTracePn_Support_bot

This Privacy Policy explains how DexPN (the “App”) handles information when you use the iOS application. DexPN is a VPN client for iOS that uses Apple VPN / Network Extension capabilities to route network traffic through a selected server.

---

## 1) Information We Process

### 1.1 Information you provide
When you contact support, we process the information you send us (for example: messages, screenshots, and any details you include).

### 1.2 Information processed to provide VPN functionality
To operate the VPN connection and keep the service stable, the App and/or VPN infrastructure may process limited technical information such as:
- Connection events (connect/disconnect time),
- Selected server/region (if applicable),
- App version and basic technical diagnostics (error codes).

### 1.3 What we do not intentionally collect
We do **not** intentionally collect:
- Your browsing history (websites you visit),
- The content of your traffic (messages, files, photos),
- The URLs you open,
- DNS queries.

> Note: To provide VPN service, network traffic passes through VPN servers. The App is not designed to inspect or store the contents of your traffic.

---

## 2) How We Use Information
We use processed information to:
- Provide and maintain VPN connectivity,
- Diagnose and fix technical issues,
- Respond to support requests,
- Protect the service from abuse and maintain security.

---

## 3) Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary:
- With service providers (hosting/infrastructure) solely to operate the service,
- If required by law and valid legal process.

---

## 4) Retention
We keep information only as long as necessary:
- Support messages: retained for a reasonable period to resolve issues and improve support quality.
- Technical diagnostics: retained for a limited time to investigate incidents and improve reliability.

You can request deletion (see Privacy Choices).

## 5) Security
We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.
